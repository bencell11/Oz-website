Bio: Oz is a 22-year-old recording artist and singer-songwriter from Boulder, Colorado. Oz is known for his highly emotional and deep songs that will make sure to connect on every level. Oz started making music at 15 and has been creating sounds ever since. From a child, his music has been connecting and resonating with his audience due to his vulnerability and his personal music style.  

Taking heavy inspiration from the punk rock scene, Oz also blends melodic rap, RnB and pop to make a sound you need to check out for yourself. With the main goal of helping and heal the broken, Oz is known for an unstoppable work ethic, so you can expect a new and unique song every couple of weeks from the Colorado native.
